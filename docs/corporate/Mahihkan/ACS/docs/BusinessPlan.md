# ACS — Business plan
ArcherCryptoSolutions Inc. (ACS) is the umbrella holding company for ACA (analytics), CVR Essentials (public records), and Mahihkan (consumer brand). IP and ownership are consolidated under the Archer Family Trust to provide governance, licensing, and investor readiness. ACS documents inter-company resource transfers at industry-norm rates to support GST/HST registration and banking readiness.
