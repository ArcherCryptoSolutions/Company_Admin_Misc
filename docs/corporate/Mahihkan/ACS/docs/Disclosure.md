# ACS — Disclosure & risk
Risks: regulatory changes (securities, AML/KYC, privacy), market volatility, counterparty risk, outages, inter-entity dependencies.
Transparency: inter-company contracts and invoices document resource transfers and fees at industry norms.
Counsel review: jurisdiction, tax, licensing, consumer rules require validation by local counsel.
